Mastermind: Rockpool Detective

Help Sammy the Seal!

The tide went out and left little Sammy stuck in a rockpool.A local seagull Gulliver knows the beach well and can guide the rescuers straight to Sammy if you can find the right rockpool!

How to play

* Identify Sammy's rockpool by finding the right combination of Rockpool Creatures!

* Start by placing four Rockpool Creatures into the bottom of the grid

* You must guess the correct creatures AND and the order they occur in.

* Clue shells are placed after each turn to give you clues! These are placed in random order.

Find the combination before the grid fills up to rescue Sammy in time. Good luck!

Hint: creatures can appear more than once!
